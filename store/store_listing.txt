APP TITLE (max 50 chars):
SnapFit: Block Fit Puzzle

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Fit every pre-cut piece back into the blueprint. 150 satisfying puzzles!

FULL DESCRIPTION:
📐 EVERY PIECE HAS ITS PLACE. 📐

SnapFit hands you a blueprint and a tray of odd-shaped pieces. Your job: fit every single piece back into the grid until the blueprint is perfectly filled. No rotation, no tricks — every piece is pre-cut to fit. Finding WHERE is the puzzle.

🧩 HOW TO PLAY
• Tap a piece in the tray to pick it up
• Tap the blueprint where it should go — it snaps right in
• Tap a placed piece to lift it back out
• Fill every cell of the blueprint to win
• Place each piece right the first time for 3 stars!

✨ FEATURES
• 150 puzzles: Easy 6×6, Medium 7×7, Hard 8×8 blueprints
• Every level is solvable by construction — the pieces ARE the board
• Forgiving tap-to-place: pieces snap into any spot they fit
• Clean drafting-table blueprint aesthetic with vivid pieces
• Par system rewards first-try precision
• Calm ambient soundtrack
• Haptic feedback
• 100% offline, no ads, no purchases, zero data collection

🧠 PACKING PUZZLES, PURIFIED
Like a jigsaw with no picture and no rotation — just shapes, space, and spatial reasoning. Early boards click together in seconds; later ones will have you staring at one stubborn gap.

Grab the tray. The blueprint is waiting. 📐
